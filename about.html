Grassroot Product commenced in the year 2021. We peddle purely Made in India products providing you with an extensive variety, ranging from organic farmer products to leather jackets, boots, accessories, and casual clothing. We aim to provide best to our customers with authenticity and minimal pricing. Also we bestow some percent of our profit to our soldiers. Forging our own trendy designs and completely in sourcing casual wears. Best quality of leather with an assortment of designs. We create a buyer-centric atmosphere that completely revolves around our customers and always ready at their service.

Director

Mr. Sunil Sangwan, the director of the Grassroot hub devoted twelve years of his life in the service of the Merchant Navy.
